Design Vision

To me, Washington State represents a balance between nature and technology.

Growing up, I was often inspired by local tech innovators and the stunning landscapes of the Pacific Northwest. This instilled in me a drive to understand the intersection of nature and technology, and now I seek to manifest the relationship between the two in all my personal and professional endeavors. For my portfolio, I aspire to reflect the balance between nature and technology by leveraging design features such as earth tones, Devanagari typeface, and a blend of smooth and crisp lines.
